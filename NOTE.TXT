Welcome to Big Sticky Note!

Click Win-N to open this window.  ESC to close it.
No need to save - The note is saved automatically.


You can enter reminders, using the @time notation.   When the time comes, a reminder will appear.  Please use one reminder per line.
For example:

@Monday Go to the office
@Friday Go home!
@12:00 Remind me to eat lunch daily

Now, Feel free to delete all of this, and enter your own notes.
